What's the Club About?

Conversation Practice

We try to keep our meetings nur auf Deutsch (no English). However all levels are welcome! Feel free to come sit in and listen to get a feel for our conversations.

Books and Games

Sometimes we'll talk about a German book we're reading outside the club or current events in Germany and Europe. Othertimes, we'll play a game like Settlers of Catan or Cards Against Humanity.

Movie Nights

Once in a while, when we're not being social hermits and cramming for mid-terms or finals, we have a movie night!

Food and Beer

This wouldn't be a true German club without German style food and beer! Oftentimes, we'll set our meetings to the local restaurants and coffeeshops.
